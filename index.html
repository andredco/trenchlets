The loop

How it works.

It's a team sport. Every member's contribution stacks toward the same house yield.

01

Pick your house

10 real pump.fun communities. Walk to a totem, press E, and claim. Hold their token in your wallet to qualify.

02

Play the boards

Walk to your billboard, press E. Pick a game, pick a difficulty, play 10 stages that get harder each round.

03

Boost house yield

Your score becomes contribution %. Higher difficulty = bigger yield. Cooldown 1-2h after — keeps it skill, not spam.

04

Vault drains every 6h

15% of the vault drains each epoch. Houses with more accumulated yield earn more. Holders above 50K $TRENCHLETS qualify.

05

Events flip the table

Solar Eclipse 2x. Raid Hour 1.5x. Spotlight 3x. Earthquake 0.5x. Strategy and timing matter.

06

Stack tiers, climb up

Shrimp → Fish → Dolphin → Shark → Whale. Higher tiers unlock raids, multipliers, and bigger yield ratings.

Central vault

Built for the long run.

The vault is a developer wallet. The $TRENCHLETS token launches through it. Every 6 hours, 15% drains and ships to qualifying holders pro-rata, weighted by tier. The frontend can only read — it cannot withdraw.
