HOW TRENCHLETS WORKS

Welcome to Trenchlets

01

The City & the Houses

Trenchlets is the city. 10 launch houses live inside it, each representing a pump.fun community. Walk to a totem and press E to claim a house (you need to hold that house's token in your wallet).

02

The Central Vault

Every token bought from a house flows into the central vault. TRENCHLETS holders at higher tiers unlock more powerful tasks that pull bigger buybacks into the vault.

03

Three reward streams

Each task resolves into three splits: a slice fills the house vault, a slice routes to your personal share, and a slice funds the Trenchlets protocol treasury. Cards show the live split.

04

12 hour claim lock

Realistically, nobody should be getting paid in the first 12 hours. Your share accumulates but the vault won't release it until the lock expires from your first play. House vaults still grow meanwhile.

05

Strategy: raids & disasters

SHARK+ tier holders can walk to a rival house billboard, press E, and RAID a slice of their vault into yours. Natural disasters (fire, lightning, earthquake, locusts) hit random houses — citizens can bucket-brigade fires.

06

Town meeting

New houses are added by vote. Open MEETING, paste the CA, and cast your vote. One vote per hardware id. Top voted houses get promoted.

07

Founder-controlled vault

The on-chain vault address is a multisig the founder controls and cannot be drained from the client. This frontend never holds private keys. Once real money is in there, no one can siphon it.

08

Simple view + accessibility

Click SIMPLE for a text mirror of everything happening live — vault, houses, events, your share, the next reward window. Same data, accessible format.
